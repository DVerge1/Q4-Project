Artist Spotlight

Elton John


Elton John is a well renowned British artist, known for his hits such as "Rocketman" and "I'm Still Standing," he is a dearly beloved artist for people around the world. On January this year during the 75th Primetime Emmy Awards, he recieved and award outstanding variety special, making him the 19th person to become an EGOT, a winner of the Emmy, Grammy, Oscar, and Tony awards.

Unfortunately, he was unable to receive the reward personally as he had a knee surgery on the day, his husband David Furnish and his producer were the onese who had recieved the reward in his place.

Elton John has now won 5 Grammy Awards, two Academy Awards, one Tony award, and two Oscars. He is one of the few people to join the EGOT list.
